About Duby LLC

Founded in 2012, Duby LLC emerged from a simple vision: to revolutionize the aluminum welding industry by combining cutting-edge technology with uncompromising craftsmanship. What started as a small operation with a single welding station has grown into a state-of-the-art facility serving hundreds of clients across manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors. Our founder, Sam Duby, brought decades of welding expertise and a passion for excellence that continues to define our company culture today. We've invested heavily in the latest welding equipment, training programs, and quality control systems to ensure we remain at the forefront of the industry.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to be the most trusted aluminum welding partner for businesses that refuse to compromise on quality, speed, or reliability. We believe that exceptional welding should be accessible, affordable, and delivered with integrity. Every member of our team—from our certified welders to our project managers—shares a commitment to customer satisfaction and continuous improvement. We stand behind every weld we produce, and we measure our success not just in completed projects, but in the lasting partnerships we build with our clients. Whether you're a Fortune 500 company or a growing startup, we treat your project with the same level of dedication and expertise.
